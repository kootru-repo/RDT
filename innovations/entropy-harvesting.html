Entropy Differential Harvesting

Energy from the M⁺/M⁻ drainage flow

Long-term Research Direction: This innovation requires advances in understanding the M⁺/M⁻ boundary physics. It represents the logical endpoint of RDT applied to energy extraction and illustrates the full theoretical scope of the framework.

Executive Summary

In RDT, entropy flows continuously from M⁺ to M⁻ (or vice versa) through the boundary Σ. This drainage current $J^\mu$ drives gravity, time, and thermodynamics. If we could tap this flow at or near Σ, we might extract energy from the cosmological structure itself.

The RDT Insight

The universe isn't "running down" — entropy transfers between dual manifolds. Total entropy $S^+ + S^- = const$. The drainage IS energy transfer. If accessible, this represents a potentially inexhaustible energy source.

2.2 Energy in the Flow

If entropy transfer corresponds to energy transfer (as in thermodynamics: $dU = TdS$), the drainage current carries power:

$$P = T \cdot \dot{S}_{drainage} = T \cdot \nabla \cdot J^\mu$$

The question: Can we tap this power? Or is it fundamentally inaccessible?
