Lemma (Solid Angle from Metric Structure)

1. In spherical coordinates, the metric element on a unit sphere is: $d\Omega = \sin\theta \, d\theta \, d\phi$
2. Integrating over the full sphere: $$\Omega = \int_0^{2\pi} d\phi \int_0^{\pi} \sin\theta \, d\theta = 2\pi \cdot [-\cos\theta]_0^{\pi} = 2\pi \cdot 2 = \class{pi-highlight}{4\pi}$$
3. The two factors of π come from:
